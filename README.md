# DYOGA-Sync-App
This is Team NOVA's prototype for Business hackathon.
The DYOGA Sync app is the "Performance Operating System" for high-achieving professionals, designed to bridge the gap between mental ambition and biological capacity. 
It transforms passive biometric data into actionable business intelligence to optimize focus and prevent burnout.
Core Innovation: Active InterventionsUnlike traditional trackers, the app doesn't just display data; it actively manages your state.
Predictive Burnout Warning: Analyzes subtle heart rate and temperature shifts to alert you up to 48 hours before mental exhaustion or illness sets in.Haptic Command Center: Sends real-time "breath-guide" and "stress-nudge" vibration patterns to the Aura Ring via BLE when a stress spike is detected.Nervous System Balance Score: Replaces generic fitness metrics with specialized scores for Calm, Focus, Recovery, and Emotional Stability.
Key Features for High-PerformanceBiological 
Bandwidth Meter: 
A real-time indicator of your "remaining mental energy" calculated from sleep quality and nervous system strain.
Focus-Lock Integration:
A one-tap mode that silences notifications, sets a "Do Not Disturb" status on Slack, and plays ambient soundscapes tailored to your fatigue level.Optimal Focus Windows: Identifies the next 90-minute window where your nervous system is perfectly balanced for deep, concentrated work.
Live Bio-Streaming:
Provides high-fidelity insights from the ring's 18-path sensor array, including real-time HRV and respiratory rates.The Enterprise Advantage (ROI)The app is positioned as a measurable business solution rather than an HR perk.
Corporate "Team Readiness" Dashboard: 
An anonymized tool for leadership to monitor collective burnout risks and optimize team workloads based on physiological data.
Expertise-Driven Logic:
The app's recovery protocols and breathing cycles (like Box Breathing and 4-7-8 Calm) are built on Diksha Agwan's professional expertise in yogic science.
